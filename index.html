Brief Introduction

Dr. Jiaxin Chen received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Applications Technology from Beihang University in 2017. From 2017 to 2018, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow at New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD). From 2018 to 2021, he was a research scientist at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI). His current research interests include computer vision (especially video and image analysis and 3D computer vision) and machine learning (especially metric learning and learning to hash). He has authored/co-authored over 10 papers in top conferences/journals such as IEEE Transactions on Image Processing (TIP), CVPR, ICCV and ECCV.

Research Interests

  • Imagery/Video Analysis: Object Re-Identificatioin/Search/Retrieval；Compressed Video Analysis

  • Machine Learning: Self-supervised learning；Learning to hash；Metric learning

  • Deep Model Compression: Model compression；Light-weight deep model

  • 3D Computer Vision: 3D object representation/recognition/retrieval

  • Visual Perception for Unmanned Systems: Object detection/tracking
